Focaccia wit Caramelized Onion , tart
Cranberries, and brie

Wow your taste breds with this fetive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingradients

For the focaccia:
  • 4cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pans
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons oliveoil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
